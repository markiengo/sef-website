    Saigon Equities Forum

    Independent research into Vietnam’s macroeconomy and capital markets

    Join Us

    Saigon Equities Forum (SEF) is a student-led financial research platform with a focus on Vietnam. We produce comprehensive research reports and insights on Vietnam’s macroeconomy and capital markets, including listed companies and industries on the VN-Index.

     

    Consisting of two Macro and Equity Research teams, SEF brings together students from renowned institutions spanning across Asia, the US, Europe, and Australia, under one common purpose to study, analyze, and promote Vietnam’s financial and geopolitical landscape.

    Vietnam Healthcare Sector February 2026

    Vietnam’s healthcare sector is in the midst of an expansion phase, with the market projected to nearly double by 2030, supported by an aging population, rising income levels, and accelerating healthcare privatization. The report examines the undercapacity of public hospitals, the policy targets to raise the number of private beds, notable M&A activity, and a fast-growing medical tourism market targeting $4B by 2033.

    SBV Note: FX Stabilisation Sacrifices

    Vietnam welcomes the Horse Year with a strong domestic demand, but with the attached vulnerability of external macro conditions. A report from our Macro Analyst Tue studies the State Bank’s prioritization of FX stability over targeting inflation amid concerns of credit dependence and global financial conditions, and the trade-offs associated with it.
